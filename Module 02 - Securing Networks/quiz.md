## Question 9

**Match the type of hackers to the description.**

| Category | Correct description |
|---|---|
| Black hat hackers | They are unethical criminals who violate computer and network security for personal gain, or for malicious reasons, such as attacking networks. |
| White hat hackers | They are ethical hackers who use their programming skills for good, ethical, and legal purposes. They may perform network penetration tests to compromise networks and systems by using their knowledge of computer security systems to discover network vulnerabilities. |
| Gray hat hackers | They are individuals who commit crimes and do arguably unethical things, but not for personal gain or to cause damage. An example would be someone who compromises a network without permission and then discloses the vulnerability publicly. |
